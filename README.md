# English

### 👋 About Me

QA Engineer focused on **test automation and software quality analysis**. I am particularly interested in **requirements analysis, test design, and systematic defect detection**.

Before moving into QA, I worked as a **technical documentation quality controller on large industrial projects**, which helped develop **structured thinking, attention to detail, and the ability to analyze complex specifications**. These skills help me design clear and reliable tests.

I use **Java as my primary automation language** due to its strong ecosystem for testing and wide adoption in commercial development.
My goal is to grow as a **QA Automation Engineer** and contribute to building **stable and maintainable automated testing solutions**.
